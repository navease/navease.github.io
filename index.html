About NavEase

NavEase is a powerful browser extension that simplifies your web navigation by allowing you to use custom keywords to quickly access your favorite websites.

Key Features

  • Custom keyword shortcuts for your favorite URLs
  • Quick access through the browser's address bar
  • Secure login to your personal NavEase configuration
  • Easy-to-use settings panel
  • Works seamlessly across different devices

How to Use

  1. Install the NavEase extension for your browser
  2. Click on the NavEase icon and enter your configuration URL
  3. Log in with your credentials
  4. Start using keywords in your address bar by typing "navease" followed by a space and your keyword

Benefits

  • Save time by quickly accessing frequently visited websites
  • Improve productivity with custom shortcuts
  • Enjoy a clutter-free bookmarks bar
  • Access your personalized navigation from any device
